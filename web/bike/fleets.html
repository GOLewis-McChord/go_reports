Check-Out

GO Bike is available for FREE to all DoD employees, JBLM contractors, and dependents 18 years of age or older. Please bring your DoD ID card to any of the check-out locations. After signing a waiver staff will check out a bike for you.

Included Gear

You are required to wear reflective gear on the installation when biking. We provide helmets as available, reflective gear, a water bottle cage, lights, and a lock. We recommend you bring your own helmet.

Safety Information

It is important to follow the rules of the road. You can view this video series for information about bicycle safety. You can also find the videos on the GO Lewis-McChord YouTube channel.
