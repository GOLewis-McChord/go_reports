Check-Out

GO Bike is available for FREE to Service members, DoD civilians, and contractors. Please bring your military Common Access Card to the check-out location. After signing a waiver staff will check you out a bike.

Included Gear

It is required you wear reflective gear on the installation. We provide reflective gear, helmets as available, a water bottle cage, lights, and the lock. We recommend you bring your own helmet.

Safety Information

It is important to follow the rules of the road. You can view this video series for information about bicycle safety.
